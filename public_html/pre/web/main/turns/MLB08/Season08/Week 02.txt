claim it was the player's decision (behind the scenes the club have told him he's going, but no-one makes it public). Equally,<L.31.1>
there are players who "want to play as long as they can", so they'll look for another year, and are prepared to take a lower wage,<L.31.1>
though how much lower is a personal decision.<L.31.1>
<L.12.1>
That's why I think there should be a random factor for all players. "He will. He won't" isn't realistic, and whilst perhaps it<L.31.1>
makes things easier for decision making, I think it's good to have a bit of uncertainty and decision making.<L.31.1>
