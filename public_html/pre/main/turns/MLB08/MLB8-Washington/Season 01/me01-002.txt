<Z>Pinch Hitters <C> I've made a software change with pinch hitters. Previously the special actions routine would reject a PINCH<L.31.1>
order if you tried to put the same player in two spots, or two players in the same spot. This supposedly not being a sensible<L.31.1>
thing to do. However, someone has now come up with good reasons to do so - first you might have a platoon at one position, and<L.31.1>
want the other half of the platoon pinch hitting for the other (so A pinches for B, and B pinches for A) and you might want a guy<L.31.1>
pinch hitting for two positions (eg SS and 2B), but not wanting to go the whole hog and use the IF and OF.<L.31.1>
<L.12.1>
The rules are unchanged, as none of this was in the rules anyway. Note that a pinch hitter can't be replaced by a second pinch<L.31.1>
hitter (if a pinch hitter comes in, he plays until the end of the game regardless) and obviously a player can't pinch hit in more<L.31.1>
than one position (the software shouldn't let this happen, but tell me if it does!). If the computer has a choice of pinch hitters<L.31.1>
then it'll go with the higher one - ie PINCH1 first, then PINCH2 and so forth.<L.31.1>
<L.12.1>
All of these changes are in the software immediately - if you spot anything awry (unlikely, but possible) please let me know.<L.31.1>
